Instructions

Our user-friendly high school GPA calculator helps you calculate your GPA with ease.

Steps

  1. Add Courses: Click 'Add Course' to enter your course details, including grade, name, and class type (Standard, Honors, or AP).
  2. Calculate GPA: Review your entered courses, then click 'Calculate GPA' to see your unweighted and weighted GPA.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use the GPA calculator for courses taken outside of my high school, such as online courses or dual enrollment programs?

Yes, you can calculate your GPA for courses taken outside of your high school, just ensure you know the weight of the courses (i.e., is it 4.0, 4.5,or 5.0).

Does the GPA calculator account for repeated courses or grade replacements?

It depends on your school's policy, or whether or not your school allows you to "erase" your first attempt at the course from your transcript.

What if I make a mistake in enering my course information?

If this happens, simply press the "Remove Course" button.
